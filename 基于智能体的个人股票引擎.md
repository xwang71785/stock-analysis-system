1. Undervalued Stock Screener

Prompt:

"Act as a professional equity research analyst.
Scan the current stock market and identify 10 undervalued companies with strong fundamentals.

Apply the following filters:
• P/E ratio below industry average
• Consistent revenue and earnings growth over the last 3 to 5 years
• Debt to equity ratio below sector median
• Positive and growing free cash flow
• ROIC above industry average
• Analyst consensus upside of at least 30 percent

For each company provide:
• Short business overview
• Why the stock appears undervalued
• Key risks
• Estimated intrinsic value range"

2. Insider Trading Pattern Analyzer

• Any potential red flags"
